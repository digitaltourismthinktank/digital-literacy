01 · The promise

Fluency, not a developer's job.

This course teaches the digital foundations underneath AI: how the web, content, media, data and models actually work. It is for marketers, strategists, managers and executives, the generalists who decide and direct, not the people who build. Every technical term is defined the first time it appears.

Follow the conversation
Understand what a developer, designer or AI vendor is really asking you, so the room never moves on without you.
Decide with judgement
Know what to buy, what to build and what to walk away from. Each tool comparison is a comparison, never a recommendation.
Grounded in real work
Every module ends in a hands-on exercise and an interactive piece built from the work you actually do, not abstract theory.

Track 4 · Data fundamentals · Free

Spreadsheets and CSVs

The reference module. At its centre is a three-view inspector: the same data shown as raw text, as a spreadsheet grid and as the AI's interpretation, with broken examples you can switch on to see exactly what trips a model up.

A CSV, comma-separated values, is a plain text data file. We define it here, then show you why the format matters more than it looks.
